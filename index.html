Type: Trojan

Alerta was created in 2006, where it spread through a fake email translated from Portugese to:

Alert! Your computer has been infected by a virus! This email is from Microsoft Team.
To delete the malware, please open this file (alerta.exe file)

"A HOMOSEXUAL TRYING TO USE THE COMPUTER WAS DETECTED!! DO YOU HAVE ANYTHING TO DO WITH IT?"

There's a catch though. As soon as you try press the "NO!!!" button, it moves away from the cursor.
Even trying hitting the Enter key while having the button selected will not work.
Simply, you get forced to press yes.
